Updates

We are moving into the first round of beta testing this month!

nOCD is a mobile platform that allows for the self-treatment and monitoring of Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD) symptoms.

We are Taking advantage of the cutting edge technology in the new generation of smartwatches to bring you on-the-go Exposure Response Prevention treatment and keep you synced with your specialists from anywhere in the world.

Discreet "On The Go" Self Monitoring

OCD doesn't always strike at convenient times. With nOCD, you can do your Exposure Therapy anywhere, all while collecting data for smarter OCD treatment decisions.

Objective Data Collection and Analysis

While the user is guided through Exposure Therapy on the watch, nOCD will automatically collect biometric data from the episode! The app then helps you or your therapist analyze the data for better OCD treatment and Exposure Therapy decisions.

Structured, Daily, ERP Plans

Studies show that the only way to become fully treated for OCD is through Exposure and Reponse Prevention Therapy (Exposure Therapy or ERP) One of the biggest challenges with today's treatment is that OCD sufferers must find the courage to follow their own treatment program outside of therapy. nOCD aims to make this is easy as it can be. They can create an organized ERP plan, set reminders telling them when to start treatment, view custom prompts based on their trigger hierarchy, receive input from their therapist, and more!
